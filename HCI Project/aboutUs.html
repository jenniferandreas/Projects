HISTORY


POWithV is a platform that providing customers with an easy, fast, and secure shopping entrusted goods service in Indonesia.

POWithV aims to continually enhance its platform and become the region’s e-commerce destination of choice. POWithV has a wide selection of product categories ranging from consumer food to medicine.

POWithV was first launched in Jakarta in 2018, and has since expanded its reach to Singapore, Malaysia, and Thailand.

Our mission is to better the lives of consumers and small businesses with technology, and is listed on the NYSE.

Our team believes in the transformative power of technology and wants to change the world for the better by providing a platform to connect buyers and our worker in one community.
